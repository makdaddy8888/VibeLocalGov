What this is for

Many Australian council staff already have Microsoft 365 licences that include Copilot and Power Platform (Power Apps, Power Automate, Dataverse). The hard part is describing the problem so an assistant can help you design a responsible, reviewable app or flow.

This page collects your work context and biggest process pain, then writes a single prompt you can paste into Vibe in Power Platform (or your org’s equivalent experience). Do not paste resident names, addresses, or other personal information; describe processes and data types in generic terms.

    Privacy & records

    When you click Generate Vibe prompt, your answers and the generated prompt are stored in this browser only (local storage) and shown in Responses captured — nothing is sent to our server. If you export, email, or share, do so in line with your council’s privacy and official records rules. For formal programs, your organisation should issue a collection notice where required.
